What is Sol's RNG?

Sol's RNG is a game centered around the Roll button at the bottom of your screen, the main purpose of the game is testing your luck and going for the rare auras this game has to offer. Each roll is a chance to obtain one of countless auras, with some being far rarer than others. The goal of the game is to collect auras. To improve your chances, you can acquire gauntlets and potions, which can be earned or crafted as you progress. Show off your luck with the auras you collect, or use them to craft even rarer ones.
